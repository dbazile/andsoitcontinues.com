     For all of those who think that our clan is new, that we were born when the first RTCW demo came to public, you're completely wrong. Our Clan exists, officially, since 1997, when we started playing Half Life's MOD Team Fortress. Back then, online multiplayer FPS were relatively new, specially in Portugal where the best connection you could get was 33,6Kbps. You can imagine that was really hard to play internet games smoothly, even so we were not intimidated by everyone's else ping (even if it was 10 times lower than ours) and fought hard and bravely to kick ass like very few were capable to.

     That brings us to the birth of our Clan's name... In Half Life TF, we played mostly as snipers, and believe us, we were really good at it. But due to a number of problems we were forced to step down the online games.

     It's common knowledge that RTCW is not the first Wolfenstein, and the founders of SS play computer games long before Wolfenstein 3D and Wolfenstein Spear of Destiny were released. We remember well that date, like it was yesterday, back then we played Wolfenstein hours after hours, and that is why we could not stay indifferent while assisting to the release of another excellent sequel of that great game, the grandfather of all FPS. And so, after making some efforts, we returned, we came back from the ashes, better than ever, and not just as snipers...

     For the first time SharpShooters has a web page and for the first time we feel ready to start the recruiting process. For many years SharpShooters were away from the international scene but now we are back with the acclaimed title Return to Castle Wolfenstein, and we are totally convinced that enemy online gamers will crawl to our feet.
